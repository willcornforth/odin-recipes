How to make: Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni pizza is an iconic and universally loved dish that brings together the simple pleasures of gooey melted cheese, zesty tomato sauce, and the irresistible flavor of pepperoni. This beloved pizza variety is a true comfort food that satisfies cravings and delivers a perfect balance of textures and tastes in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

How to cook

  1. Preheat the oven to 232 degrees celsuis.
  2. Prepare the dough.
  3. Transfer the dough to a stone or pan.
  4. Brush the dough with olive oil.
  5. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  6. Add cheese and pepperoni.
  7. Season and flavour the pizza with oregano and chilli flakes.
  8. Bake the pizza in the oven for 12-15 minutes.
  9. Garnish and serve with basil leaves.
  10. Enjoy!
